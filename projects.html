Yard Hopper

Yard Hopper is your go-to tool for finding garage sales tailored to your interests! Explore our interactive map and home feed to discover sales near you, filter by radius or category, and save your favorite posts to plan the perfect weekend route.

Hosting a sale? Create detailed listings with photos and item categories to attract the right buyers and boost visibility. Whether you're hunting for treasures or drawing a crowd, Yard Hopper makes garage sales easy, efficient, and community-focused!

Tools Used

Front End: Figma, React Native, Expo, Tailwind CSS

Back End: Express API, Firestore NoSQL DB, Firebase

Management: ClickUp, Discord
